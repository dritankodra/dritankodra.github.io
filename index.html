About Me

I finished my undergraduate studies in Physics at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2010. I then continued my studies in the Masters program of Computational Physics at the same institution, from which I graduated in 2012. As a next step I continued with a PhD degree in Physics and Astronomy at the University of Pittsburgh in the US, which I obtained in 2018. Since then I returned to Greece where I reside with my wife, and I have been employed as a data scientist by the company "To the point" in Thessaloniki, where I have had full responsibility for the design of public opinion surveys as well as the collection and analyzing of the data. Furthermore, I have been collaborating with the Department of Chemistry of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, where I have been performing duties as a Biostatistician by gathering metabolomic data from a variety of foods from the literature.
