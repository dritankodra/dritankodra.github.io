About Me

I received my bachelor's degree in Physics from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTh) in 2010, and my Master's degree in Computational Physics at the same institution in 2012. I continued my academic career with the PhD program in Physics and Astronomy at the University of Pittsburgh (UPitt) in Pennsylvania, US, receiving my title in 2018. I am currently engaged as a postdoctoral researcher collaborating with the "Biomic_AUTh" research group being involved in a variety of projects such as the design, development and deployment of food composition databases, the meta-study of literature on issues regarding metabolite annotations using text mining techniques, as well as the development of Machine Learning models for predictions of retention times from LC-MS experiments. I have simultaneously been employed as the Lead Data Scientist by the Market Research company named "To the point". My responsibilities entail the design and implementation of Market Reasearch and Public Opinion Surveys, as well as the collection and analysis of data, where I have led the effort of developing in-house computational and statistical tools of analysis and automation.
